Welcome to Polyviou Family Dentistry

At Polyviou Family Dentistry, we pride ourselves on providing top-notch dental care to patients in Lincoln Park, Michigan, and surrounding areas. Led by our experienced dentist, Dr. Paul Polyviou, our dedicated team is passionate about creating beautiful smiles and ensuring the comfort of every patient. With over 25 years of experience in the field, we have honed our skills in helping our patients overcome dental anxiety and making each visit to our office a positive one

We understand that financial concerns can often be a barrier to receiving necessary dental treatment. This is why we offer flexible payment options and handle insurance billing for your convenience. Your smile is important to us, so schedule a free consultation with us today to learn more about how we can help you achieve optimal oral health.

Meet the Doctor

Dr. Paul Polyviou

Dr. Paul Polyviou has been practicing dentistry since 1995. Raised in the downriver area, Dr. Polyviou received his undergraduate degree from Wayne State University and earned his dental degree from the University of Detroit Mercy School of Dentistry.

Dr. Polyviou is truly committed to providing the highest quality dental care to his patients. As such, he spends countless hours outside of the office pursuing continuing education courses so that he can maintain the best dentistry for all his patients.
